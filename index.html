About Me

I am a Master's student in Environmental Science at the Central University of Rajasthan, with a strong focus on using Satellite Remote Sensing (SAR & Optical) to understand environmental and cryospheric changes.

My research journey has taken me from the labs of IIT Bombay, where I studied CO2 sequestration, to the Space Applications Centre (ISRO), where I characterized Himalayan glacier dynamics using high-resolution radar data.
