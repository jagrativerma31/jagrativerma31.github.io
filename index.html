About Me

I am a Master's student in Environmental Science at the Central University of Rajasthan, with a strong focus on using Satellite Remote Sensing (SAR & Optical) to understand environmental and cryospheric changes.

My research journey has taken me from the labs of IIT Bombay, where I studied CO2 sequestration, to the Space Applications Centre (ISRO), where I characterized Himalayan glacier dynamics using high-resolution radar data.

With a UGC NET qualification (AIR 211) and a background in both field investigations and machine learning (Random Forest, TensorFlow), I am seeking PhD opportunities to further explore the intersection of climate change, geospatial analysis, and earth system science.

Outside the lab, I am an avid nature photographer, capturing the landscapes I study.

Research Experience

Himalayan Glacier Characterization (ISRO)

Space Applications Centre, ISRO | 2025

Analyzed seasonal glacier dynamics using RISAT-1A SAR backscatter and LISS-III/IV optical data. Developed a Random Forest model to classify snow-ice facies and optimized SAR preprocessing using Frost filters to preserve edges. Linked facies changes to ERA5 temperature trends.

CO2 Sequestration using Basalt

IIT Bombay | 2022

Investigated enhanced rock weathering for carbon capture. Prepared basalt samples under controlled pH and incubated with CO2. Used X-Ray Diffraction (XRD) to identify calcium and magnesium carbonate mineral formation, validating sequestration potential.

Glacier & Lake Dynamics in Sikkim

M.Sc. Minor Project | 2025

Assessed the retreat of the Zemu Glacier (2013-2023) using Landsat NDSI. Analyzed terrain (Slope, Aspect) and long-term ERA5 climate data to evaluate susceptibility to Glacial Lake Outburst Floods (GLOFs).

Climate Variability in Beas Basin

B.Sc. Final Project | 2024

Quantified Land Use/Land Cover (LULC) changes using Sentinel-2 imagery. Performed regression trend analysis on ERA5 temperature/precipitation data, finding a correlation of r=-0.55 between LULC changes and climatic variability.
